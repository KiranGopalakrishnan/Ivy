Hutch

Now seamlessly sync with your ftp server for effortless change commiting

Hutch enables you to upload your changes in your projects to your host via an FTP Account on your server .
Manage multiple projects and sync with your host via hutch
Allows you to control which folders/files it should not be checking for changes
Handles the creation of folders automatically
